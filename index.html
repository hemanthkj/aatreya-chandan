ABOUT US

Aatreya Controls And Technologies

Aatreya Controls and Technologies have come into being in the year 2009. We are a sole proprietorship company, engaged in manufacturing, supplying and exporting a comprehensive range of Core Taping Machines, Dip Soldering Machines, Mechanical Tensioners, Magnetic Tensioners, Electronic Tensioners, Servo Tensioners, Tension Meters, Wire Jump Preventer, Ceramic Pulley Wheels and many others. We have built large customers’ base relatively in very short period of time. All these have enabled us earning immense recognition all across the nation. We work in close proximity with our clients, to understand their specific needs and provide solutions accordingly. For this, we welcome the suggestions of our clients and try to implement the same in our product development procedure. Our enduring relationship with customers proves our commitment to the industry and business. As a result, we have built large customers’ base relatively in very short period of time.
